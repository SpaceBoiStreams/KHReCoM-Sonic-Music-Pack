This mod replaces all the music in the game with various Sonic the Hedgehog tracks, mostly the same used in the KH1 Sonic Music Pack, but this game has a few unique bops, which I hope I replaced well.
As a heads up, I chose -most- of the songs to fit the mood of the song it's replacing, not specifically trying to tie character themes to other character themes (Amy for Kairi, Shadow for Riku, yadda yadda)
Only songs that shouldn't be replaced are the FMV songs. Which are... literally all the cutscenes... I do not have the time or patience to change that.
